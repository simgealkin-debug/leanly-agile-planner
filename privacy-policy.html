Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2, 2025

Introduction

Leanly: Agile Planner ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Data Collection and Storage

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All data created and used within the Leanly: Agile Planner app is stored exclusively on your device.

This includes:

  • Task lists and task details
  • Task history and statistics
  • App settings and preferences
  • Pomodoro timer data
  • Any other information you enter into the app

No Account Required

Leanly: Agile Planner does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information. The app works entirely offline and does not connect to any servers.

No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use any analytics tools, tracking technologies, or third-party services that collect information about your usage of the app. We have no way of knowing how you use the app or what data you store in it.

In-App Purchases

If you make an in-app purchase through the App Store, Apple processes the transaction. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple's privacy policy applies to payment transactions.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app. If you reinstall the app, all previous data will be permanently deleted unless you have a device backup that includes the app data.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children. Since we do not collect any information, this is not applicable, but we want to make it clear that our app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
